digital garden (noun)

definition: a plot on the internet where janice plants, nurtures, and catalogs her ideas.

example usage: "sorry, i can't make it to your dog's birthday party — my digital garden is really in need of weeding."

etymology: the seed for this garden was planted in 2021 by my former coworker and fellow web gardener kevin mcgillivray. it's been germinating for about 5 years. now the work takes root.

inspiration:

  • the desire to make more and consume less more mindfully
  • the flow state i'd enter when coding xanga blog themes in my teens
  • the belief that writing is thinking
  • the joy i get from actual gardening and from wringing a metaphor to death
  • the fact karly hartzman (of the band wednesday) does what she wants on her website
  • the fear of dying without having tried
  • the internet friends i made along the way
